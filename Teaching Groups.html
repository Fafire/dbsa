Prof. HUO Huan

Professor HUO Huan is an associate Professor at School of Optical-Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Shanghai for Science and Technology. Graduated from Northeastern university, Professor HUO mainly engages in computer software and theory teaching. Her research interests include cloud data management technology, data stream query optimization, XML data management technology, etc. Professor HUO taught at the Department of Computer Information System, University of the Fraser Valley in Canada for one year, and visited the University of Waterloo as an visiting scholar f or one year. As the project director, Professor HUO is in charge of the "uncertain data stream objects on the key techniques of cluster management" of national natural science funds of China and other municipal grants, and published more than 10 papers of database theory and technology topics both in journals and conferences home and abroad. Professore Huo also served as PC member and reviewer of WAIM 04, 05, APWeb DASFAA 05, CUTE 10, NDBC10, etc, and directed the students to win the second prize of the 32th World Odyssey of the Mind China Finals, the first prize of National Software Development Contest and other awards.

Prof. CHEN Qing-kui

Professor CHEN Qing-kui, PhD supervisor, vice dean of the School of Optical-Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Shanghai for Science and Technology. He is the director of the Institute of Network Computing and Internet of Things, CCF senior member, China Distributed and Parallel Computing committee member, Shanghai CSCW committee member, the National Natural Science Fund evaluation expert, Shanghai Science and Technology Committee experts. Research interests include: parallel theory and parallel database, Internet of things and computer fleet. He has 8 years’ experience of teaching database principle, total 494 teaching hours; 5 years’ experience of teaching the principle and application of Agent and high-performance computing, total 500 teaching hours. He has directed two National Natural Science Fund projects, two key research projects of Shanghai Science and Technology Committee, "Two databases, One Network " project as one of the major project of Shanghai Natural Science Foundation, the Natural Science Foundation of Heilongjiang province. He is the major member of one National 863 Project, one National Outstanding Youth Fund, one National Education Committee Fund for outstanding study abroad returnees; He has one Shanghai Invention of Technology prize, one provincial science and technology progress award, one provincial education committee of science and technology progress award, etc.; more than 70 times publications on the core journal (journal of communication, computer research and development, computer science, etc.) ; cited several times on SCI, EI, ISTP.
